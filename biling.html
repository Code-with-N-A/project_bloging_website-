What is a Bill?

A bill is a document that lists the details of a transaction, typically involving the sale of goods or services. It serves as a record of the transaction between a seller and a buyer and includes the details of the purchased items, prices, and total amount due.

Bills are important for both customers and businesses as they act as proof of purchase and provide the necessary details for payments and future reference. Here's a closer look at the key features of a Bill:

Key Features of a Bill

  • Unique Bill Number
  • Date of Issue
  • Seller and Buyer Information
  • Itemized List of Purchased Goods/Services
  • Total Amount Due
  • Tax Information
  • Payment Terms
